## Paying in another currency

On our website you've probably noticed that we offer payment for our services in the following currencies:

- Hryvnia (UAH)
- US Dollar (USD)
- Euro (EUR)

But that doesn't mean these are the only currencies you can pay with — we automatically support the following currencies, payment systems, and countries.

### Cards on an international payment network

Any card connected to an international payment system works. How can you tell if your card qualifies?

1. Your card works with international stores, and you can pay via Google Pay / Apple Pay.
2. The card's issuing network is VISA or Mastercard. *(Cards from Russian banks bearing a Visa/Mastercard logo do not work — they're actually routed through the MIR payment system.)*
3. Your bank is not under sanctions.

### Paying with cryptocurrency

Powered by **Heleket**. We support the following cryptocurrencies:

- Tether (USDT)
- USD Coin (USDC)
- Bitcoin (BTC)
- Litecoin (LTC)
- Tron (TRX)
- Ethereum (ETH)
- GRAM
- Solana (SOL)
- Binance Coin (BNB)

...and many more — the full list of supported coins is available at [heleket.com/currencies](https://heleket.com/currencies).

## What if none of these payment methods work for me?

If none of the above methods suit you (for example, you'd like to pay with an e-wallet, by phone number, or another convenient method) — we've prepared a number of alternative solutions.

> **Important!**
>
> The minimum top-up amount for alternative payment methods is **5 euros**. Part of this amount covers taxes and related accounting on our side, so these payment methods may not be eligible for a refund.

When you pay 5 euros, the full 5 euros is credited to your balance. All transactions made in other currencies or through other payment methods are tied to euros on our end.

### Can I top up less than 5 euros with an alternative payment method?

Unfortunately, no. This amount wasn't chosen at random — from it, we cover the fees for converting funds into our currency (sometimes routed through crypto wallets), and later pay taxes on it. We don't charge any additional fees on top of that.

### How do I arrange payment using an alternative method?

It's simple — open a support ticket and specify the country, bank, and currency you can pay with. We'll prepare a solution tailored specifically for you.
