Yes, we can arrange a full or partial **chargeback**.

We can only refund money if the payment was made automatically. If the payment was made through an alternative payment method, the funds are not refundable.

## How do I request a refund?

Let's split this into two cases.

### A. If the funds are on your balance

If the funds haven't been spent yet and are still on your balance, you can request a refund as follows:

- **Plata by mono** — full refund (100%).
- **Crypto platform** (currently Heleket) — a refund is possible, but fees are negotiated individually.

> **Why is that?**
>
> We don't hold funds in crypto wallets — we withdraw them to real bank accounts and pay tax on them. When requesting a refund in cryptocurrency, we can only return the amount after deducting all required taxes.

Alternative payment methods are not refundable under any circumstances.

> **Important!** A refund can only be arranged if no more than **30 days** have passed since the transaction.

### B. If the service has already been purchased

- If you bought a service and immediately realized it wasn't what you needed, or contacted support with this request right away — we'll refund **100%** of the cost to your balance.
- If the service was activated (you had time to use it and contacted us more than **72 hours** after purchase) — we'll refund **50%** of the cost to your balance, keeping the service active for a period half as long as what you paid for. *This rule only applies if no more than **14 days** have passed since the purchase.*

Funds credited to your balance can also be withdrawn — that is, you can request a refund to the account you originally paid with (as described in section A).

**The process looks like this:** you buy a service → it doesn't suit you → you request a refund → the money is first returned to your balance → then a refund is arranged to the account you paid from (per section A).
