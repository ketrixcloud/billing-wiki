This page explains how to purchase a service.

To purchase a service from our hosting platform, follow these steps:

1. [Register in your account](https://ketrix.cloud/my/reg). During registration, confirm that you have read and accepted the [offer](https://ketrix.cloud/documents/doc1-en.pdf).

2. After registration, verify your email address. Click the email verification button in the upper-right corner of the website. We will send a code to the address you used during registration. Enter that code in the window that opens.

3. Once your email is verified, choose a service that meets your needs. Open the configurator by clicking **“new service”** in your account.

4. After reviewing the plans, [open the financial center](https://ketrix.cloud/my/fin) and click **“Top up balance”**.

5. Once your balance has been topped up successfully, purchase the service you need.

After the purchase, manage your service at [panel.ketrix.cloud](https://panel.ketrix.cloud/). Use the same credentials you use for the account where you purchased the service.
