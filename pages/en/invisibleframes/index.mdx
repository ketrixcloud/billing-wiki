This page explains how to link Discord and Telegram to your account.

## Why link social accounts?

Linked social accounts let you receive the following in direct messages:

- service expiration notifications;
- notifications about ticket replies;
- purchase receipts;
- other useful account information.

When you link Discord, you automatically join the public Ketrix Discord community. There you can talk to other customers and read our hosting news, which we currently publish only there.

If you have active services, you automatically receive the **Client** role and may gain access to the private development log or client chat.

## How to link Discord

1. [Open your client area](https://ketrix.cloud/my).

2. Sign in. On the billing home page, click the account settings button in the top-right corner, to the right of your avatar.

    ![Account settings button to the right of the avatar in the client area](invisibleframes/account-settings-button.png)

3. In the window that opens, click **“Connect Discord”**.

    ![Connect Discord button in account settings](invisibleframes/connect-discord-button.png)

4. You will be redirected to the Discord authorization page. Click **“Authorize”**.

    ![Discord authorization page with the Authorize button](invisibleframes/discord-authorization.png)

5. Once authorization succeeds, Discord is linked to your account.

> **Please note!**
>
> Authorization does not give us unrestricted access to your Discord account. We receive only:
>
> - your user avatar;
> - your Discord ID (`discord_id`);
> - the email address linked to Discord;
> - permission to add you automatically to our Discord community.

## How to link Telegram

Linking Telegram works similarly and provides the same benefits and notifications.

1. [Open your client area](https://ketrix.cloud/my).

2. Sign in. On the billing home page, click the account settings button in the top-right corner, to the right of your avatar.

    ![Account settings button to the right of the avatar in the client area](invisibleframes/account-settings-button.png)

3. In the window that opens, click **“Connect Telegram”**.

    ![Connect Telegram button in account settings](invisibleframes/connect-telegram-button-redacted.png)

4. On the Telegram website, confirm that you want to continue to the app by clicking **“Open Telegram”**.

    ![Browser dialog for opening the Telegram app](invisibleframes/open-telegram-app.png)

5. After Telegram opens, the linking command runs automatically. Done — Telegram is linked to your account.
