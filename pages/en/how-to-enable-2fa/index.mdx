This page explains how to enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on your account.

> **Important!**
>
> If you lose access to your 2FA authenticator, the only way to regain access to your account is by providing our hosting administration with the following:
>
> - the approximate date you registered your account (or started using our services);
> - payment receipts (the more, the better);
> - if you have Discord or Telegram linked, message us directly from those accounts (our team will provide the contacts).
>
> If the provided information is insufficient, the administration reserves the right to refuse account recovery.

## Enabling 2FA

1. [Open your client area](https://ketrix.cloud/my).

2. Sign in. On the billing home page, click the account settings button in the top-right corner, to the right of your avatar.

    ![Account settings button to the right of the avatar in the client area](how-to-connect-socials/account-settings-button.png)

3. In the window that opens, click **“Enable 2FA”**.

    ![Enable 2FA button in account settings](how-to-enable-2fa/enable-2fa-button.png)

4. Scan the QR code with an authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Microsoft Authenticator, 1Password, or another one), or add the manual setup key by hand — you'll find it in the “Manual setup key” field.

    ![QR code screen when enabling 2FA](how-to-enable-2fa/scan-qr-code.png)

5. Once you scan the QR code or enter the key manually, your authenticator app will generate a 6-digit code that refreshes every 30 seconds.

6. Enter this code on the next screen.

    ![6-digit code entry screen](how-to-enable-2fa/enter-2fa-code.png)

7. On the next step, enter your account password and click **“Enable”** — two-factor authentication will be turned on.

    ![Password confirmation screen with the Enable button to finish setting up 2FA](how-to-enable-2fa/confirm-password.png)

Done! From now on, you'll need to enter a code from your authenticator app every time you sign in.
