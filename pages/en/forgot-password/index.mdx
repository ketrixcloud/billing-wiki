To recover your account password, do one of the following.

## 1. If you have access to your email

Use the password reset feature on the [login page](https://ketrix.cloud/my/login).

A code will automatically be sent to your email — use it to recover your password.

## 2. If you don't have access to your email

Contact technical support through one of these channels:

- Discord — [dsc.gg/ketrix-cloud](https://dsc.gg/ketrix-cloud)
- Telegram — [t.me/b2b_ketrix](https://t.me/b2b_ketrix)
- Email — **business@ketrix.cloud**

We'll work out a password reset solution with you on a case-by-case basis.

> **Please note!** If you have 2FA enabled and lost access to it, read what to do in that case on the [2FA setup page](https://ketrix.cloud/wiki/how-to-enable-2fa).
