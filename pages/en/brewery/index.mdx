## Average response time

The approximate response time for a ticket is **up to 10 minutes** in 90% of cases.

During periods of high load, or outside working hours, the response time may increase — up to **24 hours**.

We understand that every client wants a reply as fast as possible and doesn't like to wait, but this is standard support practice. We handle every issue that arises on our end and help answer any questions that come up.

## Priority support

We offer phone support, dedicated messenger channels, or any other convenient format (except in-person meetings) for the following client categories:

- B2B clients;
- partners;
- government organizations;
- banks and financial platforms.

For all other clients, support is provided through our standard channels — the ticket system.
