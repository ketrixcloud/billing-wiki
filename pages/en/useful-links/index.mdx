This page contains useful links that may come in handy while using our hosting services.

## [Control panel](https://panel.ketrix.cloud)

Our server control panel allows you to:

- start, stop, and restart your server;
- use the online file manager or connect over SFTP;
- create and delete databases;
- create, delete, and upload backups;
- add other users who work with you.

## [phpMyAdmin](https://pma.ketrix.cloud)

A convenient web interface for managing databases. It supports both visual editing and SQL queries. Imported dumps are limited to 500 MB. For larger files, we recommend Beekeeper Studio.

## [Telegram](https://t.me/ketrix_cloud)

Our Telegram channel, where we post something about once a year. We barely maintain it, but sometimes publish unique development logs that are not available on Discord.

## [Discord](https://dsc.gg/ketrix-cloud)

Our official Discord server. We actively publish customer news, maintenance notices, and hosting updates there. We recommend joining it: it is the second mirror of the website, where we duplicate important announcements and occasionally publish information that is not available on the site.

## [Cloudflare](https://cloudflare.com)

The official Cloudflare website. Cloudflare is a major domain registrar and network service provider. We recommend using it to proxy your domains. You can also purchase a domain there and add L7 (Application Layer) protection to your website.

## [Beekeeper Studio](https://www.beekeeperstudio.io/)

One of the best desktop database management applications. We recommend it when phpMyAdmin is not enough or when you work with large databases. It does not have phpMyAdmin's dump import limit.

## [GitHub](https://github.com)

One of the world's largest software development platforms. It lets you maintain commit history, run workflows, and easily roll back to earlier versions. We recommend using version control for all your projects.

## [Check-Host](https://check-host.net)

A convenient service for checking server and client IP addresses. It can identify your IP and display public information about another address, including its region, autonomous system (AS), network operator, and hostname.

## [BGP.tools](https://bgp.tools)

A service for tracking provider routes and IP addresses. It shows which networks a provider uses to route traffic to a server and the connections and peerings between autonomous systems (AS).
